There are 40 files total (covering years 1985-2024) named "holdenharris_YYYY_v20260112.nc", each file containing one year's worth of daily-averaged model results.
They include water temperature, salinity, dissolved O2, phytoplankton and nitrate concentrations, for the bottom/surface/depth-average.

All these variables are defined on a horizontal projection of size 336x564.
The first dimension (336) roughly corresponds to east/west.
The second dimension (564) roughly corresponds to north/south.
The exact longitude and latitude of the 336x564 points is stored inside the variable "longitude","latitude".
The time-axis is named "time" and is in units of "days since January 1st 1975" (with either 365 or 366 values per file).
The files also contain a copy of the model bathymetry and a copy of the model mask (indicating whether one of the 336x564 points is considered by the model to be 'land' or 'water').
